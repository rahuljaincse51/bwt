Tulsidas (the Sanskrit name of Tulsidas can be transliterated in two ways. Using the IAST transliteration scheme, the name is written as Tulasīdāsa, as pronounced in Sanskrit.

Ramcharitmanas consists of seven Kānds (literally "books" or "episodes", cognate with cantos).

Tulsidas began writing the Ramcharitmanas in Ayodhya in Vikram Samva

The Gita is set in a narrative framework of a dialogue between Pandava prince Arjuna and his guide and charioteer Krishna.
